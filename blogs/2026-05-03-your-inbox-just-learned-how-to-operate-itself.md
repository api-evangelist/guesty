<p>Guest communication is one of the biggest operational drains in property management. High message volume, constant pressure to respond quickly, and zero margin for getting the tone wrong. Every delayed response is a missed opportunity to build trust. Every missed request is a serious disruption to the guest experience and risks eroding your review score.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Until now, AI could help you draft replies. Now it runs your inbox.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-from-ai-assisted-to-ai-operated"><strong>From AI-assisted to AI-operated</strong></h2>



<p>With Guesty, AI now lives inside your guest communication — reading every message, understanding context and sentiment, and responding in your tone, in your guest&#8217;s language. This isn&#8217;t a suggestion engine. It&#8217;s an autonomous system that sends replies on your behalf, trained on your Guesty data: your listings, your reservation details, your policies, your conversation history.</p>



<p>ReplyAI Autopilot handles the messages that don&#8217;t need you. Check-in times, Wi-Fi passwords, parking instructions, directions. Autopilot resolves them instantly and accurately, so your team never has to.</p>



<p>And the messages that do need you? AI recognizes them and steps aside. Sensitive conversations, negative sentiment, low-confidence scenarios — Autopilot flags them and puts a human back in the driver&#8217;s seat.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-when-a-message-needs-action-not-just-a-reply"><strong>When a message needs action, not just a reply</strong></h2>



<p>Not every guest message is a question. Some are requests. A broken lock. A plumbing issue. A cleaning request before an early check-in.</p>



<p>Today, teams manually scan their inbox, identify these requests, and create tasks one by one. AI Task Creation changes that workflow entirely.</p>



<p>Guesty&#8217;s AI agent analyzes incoming guest messages, detects property-related issues, categorizes them by urgency, and delivers a ready-to-assign task — pre-filled with the relevant details. One click to assign. Your team gets notified instantly.</p>



<p>No more buried requests. No more overlooked maintenance issues. No more manual task creation from message threads.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-control-that-actually-means-control"><strong>Control that actually means control</strong></h2>



<p>Automation without oversight is a liability. That&#8217;s why every layer of Guesty AI is built around your rules, not ours.</p>



<p>You set the confidence threshold that determines when Autopilot can reply autonomously. You choose which properties it&#8217;s active on, during which hours, and for which message categories, such as property essentials, amenities, property rules, financials, and local information. You configure reply delays so responses feel natural, not robotic. You define custom rules for specific scenarios — how AI handles refund requests, late check-out asks, or any situation unique to your operation.</p>



<p>Tone, style, and phrasing? You control those too, across every type of conversation with communication guidelines.</p>



<p>And when guest sentiment turns negative, you get alerted immediately — so you can step in personally when it matters most.</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-why-this-is-different"><strong>Why this is different</strong></h2>



<p>Most AI communication tools bolt onto your workflow from the outside. They see fragments of your data. They maintain their own logic in their own systems. Every refinement lives somewhere else.</p>



<p>ReplyAI Autopilot is built directly into Guesty. It pulls live data from your listings, reservations, and historical conversations. Not a partial sync, but the full picture. It works consistently across languages, maintaining natural tone and preserving context accuracy. And it doesn&#8217;t stop at replies — it detects, escalates, and creates tasks inside your PMS.</p>



<p>One platform. One source of truth. No toggling between systems to manage what your AI knows.</p>



<p>This is what AI purpose-built for hospitality actually looks like.</p>
